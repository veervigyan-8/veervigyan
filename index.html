Q1. Physics - Mechanics

The acceleration due to gravity on the moon is 1/6th of that on the earth. If a body is projected upwards with velocity v on the moon, the maximum height reached by it will be:

Correct Answer: B Height ∝ v²/g, so h_moon = 6 × h_earth
